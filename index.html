Following a rigorous screening process and nearly two years of intensive training, I became a police officer. I was initially assigned to a counterinsurgency battalion, but my IT background soon led to a reassignment to the Regional Information and Communications Technology Management Division (RICTMD), where I worked as a programmer, further refining my technical skills. While exploring career growth opportunities, I discovered a Filipino cloud engineer known as Swertified on social media. His inspiring journey and expertise in AWS (Amazon Web Services) captivated me and introduced me to the transformative potential of cloud technology. I was amazed by its ability to optimize operations, drive innovation, and reshape modern businesses. This discovery shifted my focus and ignited a deep passion for cloud computing, paving the way for a new career aspiration: to become a skilled Cloud Architect. I am now fully committed to mastering AWS and leveraging it to design secure, scalable, and cost-efficient cloud solutions for diverse organizations. Although I decided not to continue my CCNA path, this pivotal moment led me to the exciting world of cloud computing, where I am determined to grow and excel as an AWS professional. My journey reflects a continuous pursuit of learning and innovation, with a clear goal of contributing to the dynamic field of cloud architecture.
